    Ancestrality

    Skinchanger

    Beast Form

    When you move, you can choose to also transform into a beast of your size. The beast form can have any appearance you choose, but the form is that of a quadruped predatory animal and once you choose it the first time, you return to this form each time you use this trait to assume your beast form. When youtransform, everything you wear and carry transforms with you and you remain in your beast form until you use an action to return to your normal form or you become unconscious. When you use an action to return to your normal form, you heal damage equal to your level. You can use this trait a number of times equal to 1 + your level and you regain expended uses when you finish an 8-hour rest. While in your beast form, you gain the following benefits and drawbacks.

  • You cannot speak.
  • You cannot make use any equipment that transforms with you.
  • You have Scent 2 (see special senses in Chapter1).
  • You have a bonus to your Health equal to your level.
  • You make rolls to attack with 1 boon.
  • You can use your tusks, claws, or teeth as a natural weapon with the Medium Melee and Finesse properties.
